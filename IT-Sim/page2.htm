Types of Computers

Corporate Accountants

  • Works out of corporate office in his own large office; no travel required.
  • Compiles and analyzes financial information for business transactions.
  • Processes massive amounts of data and calculations on sometimes short turnaround times
  • Presents findings in memos and reports
  • Needs a Windows system to run financial software and databases

Drag and drop the best suited computer for the job described above to the 'Computer Type' box. Then, select two reasons supporting your choice from the 'Reasons' column, and click Submit.
